Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game!

There are four crytals below. By clicking a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose if your total score gets above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time you start the game, it will reset the values of the crystals!
